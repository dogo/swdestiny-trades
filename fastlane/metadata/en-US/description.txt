SWD Trades gives you the tools you need for Star Wars Destiny, right at your fingertips. It includes card search by expansion or card name and allows you to track cards you have loaned or borrowed.

Features:
• The most complete cards database.
• Track cards you have lent out or borrowed from other people.
• Sort the card list alphabetically, by color or by collection number.
• Share cards with your friends.
• Universal app for iPhone and iPad.
• Ready for iOS 10.
